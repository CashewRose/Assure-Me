Welcome to Assure Me!

Assure Me is an app that helps people with their depression. Daily affirmations, or positive statements about yourself, help with confidence and self-esteem. Sometimes you just need a little bit of validation. It can be hard to remember to do daily or just hard to remember niceties about yourself. Once a day you can recieve a text message to turn their thinking around with a personal affirmation of you wrote for yourself.
